Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meat.

Preparation time
  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes
Ingredients
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables, cooked meat,herbs
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet, tilting to spread evenly.
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the edges start to set.
  5. Add your choice of fillings on one half of the omelette.
  6. Fold the other half over the fillings and cook for another minute.
  7. Slide onto a plate, cut in half, and serve hot.
